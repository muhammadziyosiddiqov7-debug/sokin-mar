Our Story

Born From a Belief
In Better Sleep

DreamNest was founded on a single truth: the quality of your sleep shapes the quality of your entire life. We partner with artisan mills across Egypt, Portugal and Japan to source materials that don't merely look beautiful — they fundamentally change how you rest.

Every thread count, every fill weight, every weave pattern is chosen with near-obsessive attention. Because you spend a third of your life in bed. Make it extraordinary.

The Founder

Alexandra Reed

CEO & Co-Founder · Est. 2019
"I spent a decade consulting for luxury hotels — Claridge's, Aman, Ritz-Carlton. Every GM asked me the same question: what makes guests sleep so well here? The answer was always the bedding. I decided to bring that magic home."

A sleep researcher turned entrepreneur, Alexandra now leads a team of 40 across New York, London, and Lisbon. DreamNest ships to 42 countries and has been featured in Vogue, Wallpaper*, and the FT Weekend.
